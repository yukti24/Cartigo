Help & FAQ

 

How to register as a vendor?

  • Register and login as a customer on the Cartigo.in web application
  • Go to your account and click on the link become a vendor.
  • Fill the application form and wait till your membership is approved by the admin.

 

How to access the vendor panel and manage the product mapping/unmapping

  • Go to your account and click on the link switch to vendor
  • Click on the menu item “Map your Products” to add the products to your account, also add the inventory details of the products at the time of mapping.
  • Click on the menu item “Mapped Products” view/delete the products from your account, also make changes to the inventory details for respective products.

 

How to switch account from vendor to customer?

  • Go to your account and click on the link switch to customer
  • Enjoy the shopping as a customer from the wide range of listed products.

 

Is Becoming a vendor a paid service?

  • No!! for activating the vendor membership there is one-time payment fee of Rs 5000, which will be used for training the vendors to use the backend and assisting the vendors to list their product on Cartigo.in for sale.
  • Once you are at Cartigo.in, you will be ensured for 24*7 support and quality training from the experts in the industry.

 

How customers can pay for purchased products?

  • Cartigo.in offers multiple payment modes, ensuring trusted payment gateway partners using secure encryption technology to keep your transaction details confidential always.
  • Customers can use the options such as internet banking, debit card/credit card payments and cash on delivery to make your purchase. Also, we accept payments made using Visa, MasterCard, Maestro and American Express credit/debit cards in India.

 

How customers can order the products and what's the delivery time?

  • At Cartigo.in, customer can do window shopping of multiple products filtering it out from the categories/subcategories, from the top trending categories on home page, exclusive categories on the home page for different products.
  • Once they like a product they can click on product to see the details and the various images.
  • Fill the quantity required and click on add to cart.
  • Select the existing address or fill the address details and checkout.
  • Make the payment. Once payment is made, customer will receive SMS/Email notification of the purchase.
  • Cartigo.in will ensure the delivery made within 72 hours to the customer doorstep.
